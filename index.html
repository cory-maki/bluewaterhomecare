Blue Water Homecare & Hospice

Compassionate, Non-Franchise Senior Care in Central Texas

Blue Water Homecare & Hospice is Central Texas's locally owned, non-franchise senior care agency — founded and led by Jennifer Prescott, RN, MSN, CDP. From daily homecare and specialized dementia support to a dedicated Parkinson's program and end-of-life hospice services, Blue Water delivers personalized, boutique care that puts your family first. Serving Austin and communities across Central Texas, including new locations in Lampasas and Marble Falls.

Who We Are

Central Texas's Most Trusted Home Care & Hospice Team

Blue Water Homecare & Hospice was built on a single belief: every family deserves care that feels personal, not corporate. Founder Jennifer Prescott, RN, MSN, CDP has devoted her career to senior care since 1995 — and as a member of the sandwich generation herself, she understands the emotional weight families carry when a loved one needs help at home.

Unlike franchise agencies, Blue Water is independently owned and operated, giving the team full control over hiring standards, training depth, and service quality. Every caregiver is held to a higher bar — and that difference shows in the outcomes families experience every day in Austin, Cedar Park, Round Rock, Georgetown, and beyond.

Whether your family needs a few hours of daily assistance or full hospice support for a loved one at end of life, Blue Water brings the same award-winning standard of care to every home it enters.
